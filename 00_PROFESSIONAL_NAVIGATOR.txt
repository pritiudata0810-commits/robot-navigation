█ THE AI BRAIN: A* PATHFINDING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The robot's "brain" is the A* (A-Star) algorithm - industry standard for 
efficient pathfinding.

How it works:
  1. Start from beginning
  2. Use Manhattan distance to estimate how far each cell is from goal
  3. Always explore most promising cells first
  4. Guarantees shortest path every time
  5. Efficient even on difficult (40% walls) grids

Why A*?
  ✓ Optimal pathfinding (guaranteed shortest path)
  ✓ Efficient (explores minimal cells)
  ✓ Professional (used in real robotics & AI)
  ✓ Scalable (works on all difficulty levels)

This proves the AI can genuinely solve complex problems!
